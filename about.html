Bishnupriya Fuels

A FNS Ventures Company · Bharat Petroleum

Bishnupriya Fuels is a Bharat Petroleum retail outlet focused on dependable, high-quality fuel for every journey. We supply petrol and diesel for everyday commuters, commercial fleets, and long-haul travel with consistent quality and transparent pricing.

We also stock lubricants and essential vehicle-care products so your car, bike, or fleet stays protected and road-ready. Our team is committed to quick service, safe fueling practices, and a clean, well-managed forecourt.

Whether you are fueling up for work, travel, or business operations, we aim to deliver reliable service and trusted Bharat Petroleum standards every time.
